Introduction¶

In this notebook, we are going to investigae IMDB movie dataset (from Kaggle). Having a quick look at this dataset, there are some correlations or trends between various characterstics of the movie. In terms of bussiness, we want to investigate what characterstic of movies would produce the highest revenue. By finding out these characterstic, the industry is able to gain more revenue and produce high quality movie. The focus of this investigation would revolve around:

"What characterstics of movie wolud generate high revenue and what are the correlations between them."

Conclusions¶

Limitations:

IDMB-dataset of 10,000 records is relatively small to discover trens within the data. While doing some cleaning data, there are more than 50% of NULL value in budget_adj, revenue_adj which are important features to make an investigation in terms of what features that generate high revenue. So, this limitation woluld reduce the reliability while we make an conclusion.

Conclusion:

As the purpose of this investigation revole around What characterstics of movie wolud generate high revenue and what are the correlations between them, we find out that:

  1. Revenue and Budget have a week positive correlation. The data suggest that within 50%, movies with higher budget tend to produce more revenue. But if we spend too much, the propbility of generating high revenue is not high
  2. There is a strong correlation between revenue and popularity. The more popular movies are, the higher revenue it produce. So, we try to find out what features that make movie more popular. First, when it comes to genres, Fantasy, Mystery and Animation are easily to become popular. Second, the popular movies often played by these casts: Robert De Niro, Samuel L.Jackson, Tom Hawkins.
  3. Movies that are directed by Steven Spielberg, Robert Zemeckis, Tim Button are produced highest revenue.
  4. Movies that generate high revenue are Adventure, Action, Fantasy, Family and Science Ficton while Drama, Romance, Comedy and Thriller generate less revenue
